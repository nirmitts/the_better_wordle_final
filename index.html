How to Play?

  • Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries.
  • Type a valid 5-letter word and press Enter to submit a guess.
  • 🟩 Green = Correct letter, Correct spot.
  • 🟨 Yellow = Correct letter, Wrong spot.
  • ⬛ Grey = Letter NOT in the word.
  • You have 5 minutes ⏱️ on the clock. If it hits zero, the game ends and the word is revealed.
  • Stuck? Use Hint 💡 to reveal a letter- you get 2 hints per game.
